The Site uses cookies in order to interact with the Customer’s browser. The cookies are not being collected as soon as the Customer leaves the Site. In general, the cookies are being use for easier navigation at the Site by the Customer as well as the most often visited pages of the Site. In addition, cookies are being used at the sections where the Customer registers at the Site, and area where the Customer updates the information.

It is the Customer’s option to restrict collection of cookies by the Site, however this may cause inability for the Customer to use certain features.
